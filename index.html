How to Become Successful in Photojournalism ...

Assembly time: 3 minutes
Awesome rating: 95% awesome

Are you interested in photography and want to know how to become a photojournalist? Just follow these five steps below to understand what you can do to get there!

  • Things You Will Need:

  • A laptop or other electronic device
  • A notebook or pad to record your findings
  • A camera
  • A quiet place to focus
  • A way to store your work
  • Creativity

  1. Research Famous Photojournalism

    Get background information on the topic by researching influential or esteemed photojournalists and their work.

  2. Study Photography

    Look at images and printed photographs to understand what they are trying to capture and why it is important

    ">

    Work to understand the purpose of photography.

  3. Get all of the Necessary Equipment

    Do research on the best equipment for your style and look into purchasing it, so you can practice.

  4. Learn How to Examine Prints and Copy Images

    Learn how to examine prints and copy images -Learn how to spot when something is off and give criticism.

  5. Build A Portfolio

    Compile all your work so it's easy to see what you have created and show others your work.
